How to Play:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD to move the snake
  • Eat the red fruit 🍎 to grow and gain points
  • Compete against computer snakes — enemies can also eat fruit
  • When a snake dies, its body turns into fruit for the others
  • Press R to reset and start a new battle
